Conpago

May 2017 - March 2018 // Brisbane, Australia

Product Designer for the Conpago Companion, an aged care device simplifying communication for the elderly. Brought in as a UX designer, I also quickly became the UI Designer/User Research Lead/Product Manager and managed a small team of design and marketing interns. During my 11 months in the startup I presented to potential investors, designed and conducted user research interviews and assisted with business development planning. My primary tasks were designing and creating high-fidelity prototypes for the tablet, mobile and desktop applications, working through several iterations of the product and communicating remotely with developers while they created the minimum viable product.

Code Camp Australia

March 2017 - December 2017 // Brisbane, Australia

Taught students ages 5 - 13 basic programming concepts through the creation of computer games during 3 and 4 day workshops. Communicated concepts in logic, design and storytelling to kids in class sizes from 9 to 26 with the help of assistant teachers. Ensuring each child had created a functional game by the end of the course by debugging all games and conducting interactive code reviews with all classes.
